Snack Collector!

You will be given a random number at the start of the game.

There are four snacks below. By clicking on a snack, you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

You win the game by matching your total score to the random number. You lose the game if your total score goes above the random number.

The value of each snack is hidden from you until you click on it.

Each time the game starts, the value of each snack will change.
